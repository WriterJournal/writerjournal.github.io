How to write a book on WriterJournal?

WriteJournal is an innovative and versatile app tailored for the Android platform. Designed to serve a myriad of writing needs, WriteJournal is your ultimate companion for capturing creative ideas, chronicling your daily experiences, and composing your next literary masterpiece.

Whether you aspire to jot down fleeting thoughts or embark on an ambitious writing project, WriteJournal provides the tools and features to support your journey. This comprehensive tutorial section is dedicated to guiding you through the full spectrum of WriteJournal's capabilities, empowering you to author and compile an entire book seamlessly from the convenience of your Android device.

Concept of book in WriterJournal

There is no explicit definition of book in WriterJournal, but creating a book is achieved by creating a folder served as a container of chapters, the structure should be as below__

--My Book (Folder):

--------chapter 1 (document)

--------chapter 2 (document)

--------chapter 3 (document)

….

Let’s start by creating a new folder served as a book

  • Navigate to the Home page, and click the new folder icon to create a new folder to serve as a book.
  • Enter book title and description
  • Add a cover image to serve as book cover page


Congratulations! Now we have our book folder prepared!

Add Chapters

Just like there is no explicit definition of book in WriterJournal, there is also no explicit definition of chapter. That sounds bad? Not really, this is intentional, as the platform is designed to be versatile and accommodate a wide range of use cases for writers of all kinds. To organize our content, we create documents that function as chapters within our book folder. Let’s get right into it!


  • Open the newly created folder “Forest house”
  • Create new document and name it as “The house in the forest” inside the created “Forest house” folder, write its content
  • Create second document and name it as “Seasonal Changes”,write its content
  • Create third document and name it as “Continuity”,write its content


Congratulations! Now we have our chapters prepared!


Compile And Export

After preparing book folder and creating chapter documents inside it, we are now ready to compile the book as a whole with the “Export” option. Please follow the steps below to compile and export your book to be read by the world!

Locate the folder “Forest house”

Click more options and select “Export”

Select Compile to PDF file

Order your chapters if needed

Click settings and navigate to Compile Settings page to control how your book should be rendered, if needed

Now click the Export button to start compiling and exporting your book!


Please note that ordering your chapters is essential in managing your book content, you can visit the Documents page to learn exactly how the ordering works in the WriterJournal app, especially the manual order option.


If you want to learn how the parameters in the Compile settings page affect your book rendering, please visit the Export And Share page for details.
